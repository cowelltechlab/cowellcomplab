Rachel has won the [Foley Scholar Award](https://research.gatech.edu/ipat/foley-endowment). It is the **highest honor** granted by **Georgia Tech's IPaT** for student excellence!

![Rachel won the Foley Scholar Award](https://research.gatech.edu/sites/default/files/Foley-Winners-smaller-edited%20%283%29.jpg)

She was recognized for her outstanding research with EXCEL students, her deep passion for creating real-world impact, and the incredible leadership in guiding a large team to make the Higher Education LLM study for individuals with intellectual disabilities possible.
A huge thank-you to all the students on the team and everyone in our **Co-Well Lab** who provided valuable feedback and support throughout her research.
So proud of Rachel and thrilled to see our work recognized!
